FAQ

Frequently asked question

How far in advance should I book?
{01}

I recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance for portrait sessions, and 6–12 months ahead for weddings to ensure availability on your preferred date.

What is included in a session?
{02}

Each session includes a pre-shoot consultation, the photography session itself, professional editing, and delivery of a curated gallery of high-resolution images.

How long until I receive my photos?
{03}

Portrait sessions are delivered within 7–10 business days. Wedding galleries are typically delivered within 4–6 weeks after the event.

Do you travel for shoots?
{04}

Yes! I'm available for travel both domestically and internationally. Travel fees may apply depending on location and duration.

What if the weather is bad on shoot day?
{05}

We'll reschedule at no extra charge if weather significantly impacts the shoot. I also embrace overcast skies — they often make for beautiful, diffused lighting.
